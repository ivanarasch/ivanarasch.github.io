Featured · M.S. Thesis · NYU Steinhardt · 2025

DJing in Latent Space

🎛️

A custom MIDI DJ controller wired directly to a 512-dimensional MusicVAE latent space, with output to a Yamaha Disklavier acoustic piano. Physical gestures — knobs, magnetic encoders, proximity sensors, a capacitive touch strip — navigate and interpolate between musical ideas in real time.

The project asks: what new forms of musical agency open up when you design both the hardware and the model? Starting from a survey of real-time neural audio style transfer approaches, the work pivoted to the MIDI domain where latent space navigation became physically intuitive.

The controller features a dual-deck layout with 14 potentiometers, encoders, and sensors. A Python/pretty_midi pipeline extracts song chunks. Web MIDI routes input to a browser-based interpolation engine, with 11-step spherical interpolation on the crossfader and attribute vector arithmetic for note density and interval control.

Installation · Friedrichstraße, Berlin · 2023

Analog Dream

🏛️

An immersive audiovisual installation at Friedrichstraße, Berlin. A Pure Data patch captured real-time body movement data from a Kinect sensor, translating audience motion into data-automated sound generation.

The piece invited visitors to become performers without realizing it — their movement through the space shaped the sonic environment around them. Over 80 people interacted with the installation across its run.

The work shares the same thread as the thesis: physical gesture as interface, the body as controller, real-time data as compositional material.

Research · Spatial Audio · NYU · 2024

Sound Event Detection + Direction of Arrival

📡

A multi-task SELD model for 4-channel First Order Ambisonics audio. STFT features computed across all channels, with Direction of Arrival estimated in 3D Cartesian coordinates. Built for CS-GY 9223 Machine Listening (Prof. Juan Pablo Bello), based on DCASE 2019 Task 3.

The model jointly learns to detect sound events and estimate their spatial position, requiring simultaneous reasoning across temporal and spatial dimensions.

Research · ML · DCASE 2023 Task 6A

Automated Audio Captioning

🤖

A dual-encoder architecture for automated audio captioning on Clotho v2, combining ConvNeXt and BEATs audio encoders with a GPT-2 decoder fine-tuned via LoRA. Best configuration (r=16, α=32, dropout=0.1) achieved SPIDEr 0.243.

Built with teammates Yujin and Mehmet Atil. Responsibilities included the SPIDEr-FL metric section, result interpretation, dependency resolution, and implementing the ClothoWithEmbeddings dataset class.

Research · Survey · 2024

Neural Audio Style Transfer for Live Performance

🌀

A systematic evaluation of Magenta RealTime, AFTER, RAVE, and DDSP for live performance contexts. Each system benchmarked on latency, timbral control, and playability.

The survey produced an honest scholarly mapping of each approach's constraints — revealing that real-time audio style transfer wasn't yet viable for expressive, low-latency physical performance. This was the direct pivot point toward the MIDI latent space approach in the thesis.
